ABOUT THE PROJECT




It is a fact that today, compared to men, fewer works of art by women end up under the auction hammer. But if women's works are sold, do auction houses correctly assess their artwork in a gender unbiased way?

This website aims to visualize the results of the thesis “Contemporary Artists under the Hammer” of Fien Messens that she published as partial fulfilment of the requirements for the Degree of Master of Digital Humanities. The graphs are coming to life by hovering over/clicking on them. You also have the option to download the graphs.

You will find a short interpretation and conclusion for each visual. More detailed information can be found in the thesis.

Feel free to contact me if you have a question or a remark. Look forward to hearing from you.
